About Us

We are a local family owned business in the New Jersey/ New York area, specializing in high-quality, interior and exterior, painting and epoxy flooring services. Our team is dedicated to providing professional and reliable service for both residential and commercial projects. Our jobs range from small touch up jobs to newly developed homes.
